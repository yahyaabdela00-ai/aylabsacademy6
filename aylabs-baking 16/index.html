No experience needed at all. Our Starter module begins from absolute zero — we teach you everything step by step in Amharic.

Any smartphone, tablet, or computer. Our platform works on all devices and is optimized for mobile — so you can watch lessons while you bake.

We accept Commercial Bank, Bank of Abyssinia, and Telebirr. After registration, send your payment screenshot to our Telegram (@AYLabs1 or @AYlabs2) or WhatsApp (0960682220 / 0990691220). We approve you within 24 hours.

Yes! All lessons are taught in Amharic with written materials available in both Amharic and English.

We open a new cohort every month. Registration closes once seats are filled — enroll now to secure your place in the next cohort.

Yes! Upon completing all modules, you receive an official AY Labs Baking School certificate signed by your instructor and AYafri Labs PLC.
